Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN):

Description: PM-KISAN provides income support to farmers by providing direct cash transfers of Rs. 6,000 per year in three equal installments to small and marginal farmers.
Website: pmkisan.gov.in

Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (PMGSY):

Description: PMGSY aims to provide all-weather road connectivity to rural areas, improving accessibility and connectivity to markets, health services, and educational institutions.
Website: pmgsy.nic.in

Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (PMAY):

Description: PMAY aims to provide affordable housing to urban and rural poor, offering financial assistance, subsidies, and support for the construction, renovation, or purchase of houses.
Website: pmaymis.gov.in

Deen Dayal Upadhyaya Gram Jyoti Yojana (DDUGJY):

Description: DDUGJY focuses on rural electrification by providing access to electricity to all rural households, agricultural pump sets, and other essential public facilities.
Website: ddugjy.gov.in

Pradhan Mantri Jan Dhan Yojana (PMJDY):

Description: PMJDY aims to ensure financial inclusion by providing banking services and access to financial products, including bank accounts, insurance, and pension schemes, to every household.
Website: pmjdy.gov.in
