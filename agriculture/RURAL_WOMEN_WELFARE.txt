National Rural Livelihood Mission (NRLM):

Description: NRLM focuses on women empowerment through self-help groups (SHGs), providing them with financial assistance, skill development, livelihood opportunities, and social support.
Website: nrlm.gov.in

Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana Yojana (PMMVY):

Description: PMMVY provides financial assistance to pregnant women and lactating mothers, aiming to improve their health and nutrition during pregnancy and early motherhood.
Website: pmmvy-cas.nic.in

Mahila e-Haat:

Description: An online marketing platform that promotes entrepreneurship and facilitates the sale of products made by women entrepreneurs, artisans, and self-help groups.
Website: mahilaehaat-rmk.gov.in

Udyogini Scheme:

Description: The scheme supports women entrepreneurs by providing them with financial assistance, skill development, and capacity-building support for setting up and expanding their businesses.
Website: khadiindia.gov.in/udyogini-scheme

Support to Training and Employment Program for Women (STEP):

Description: STEP aims to empower women by providing vocational training and employment opportunities, enhancing their skills and employability.
Website: socialjustice.nic.in/programmeschemes.php?programmeid=53
