Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY):

Description: PMFBY is an insurance scheme that provides financial assistance to farmers in case of crop damage or loss due to natural calamities, pests, or diseases.
Website: pmfby.gov.in

Paramparagat Krishi Vikas Yojana (PKVY):

Description: PKVY promotes organic farming practices by providing financial assistance and support for the adoption of organic farming methods.
Website: paramparagatkrishi.com

Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchai Yojana (PMKSY):

Description: PMKSY focuses on improving irrigation infrastructure, water use efficiency, and distribution of water resources to enhance agricultural productivity.
Website: pmksy.gov.in

Soil Health Card Scheme:

Description: The scheme provides farmers with soil health cards that contain information about soil nutrient content, enabling them to make informed decisions regarding crop selection and fertilizer usage.
Website: soilhealth.dac.gov.in

National Mission on Sustainable Agriculture (NMSA):

Description: NMSA promotes sustainable agriculture practices by integrating various initiatives, including soil health management, water conservation, and climate change adaptation.
Website: agriculture.gov.in/nmsa
