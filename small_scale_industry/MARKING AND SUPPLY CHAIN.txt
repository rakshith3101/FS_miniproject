Pradhan Mantri Kisan SAMPADA Yojana:

Description: The scheme focuses on the development of efficient and modern supply chains for agro-products, including primary processing centers, collection centers, and e-marketing platforms.
Website: pmkisan.gov.in/PmKisan/PMKSYHomePage.aspx

Market Intelligence and Early Warning System (MIEWS):

Description: MIEWS provides farmers with market-related information, including prices, demand, and supply, to help them make informed decisions regarding marketing and supply chain management.
Website: e-nam.gov.in/MIEWS

Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY):

Description: PMFBY is an insurance scheme that provides financial assistance to farmers in case of crop damage or loss due to natural calamities, pests, or diseases, thus supporting risk management in the supply chain.
Website: pmfby.gov.in

National Agricultural Cooperative Marketing Federation of India Ltd. (NAFED):

Description: NAFED plays a vital role in marketing agricultural produce by providing marketing support, procurement, and storage facilities, benefiting farmers in the supply chain.
Website: nafed-india.com

Pradhan Mantri Annadata Aay Sanrakshan Abhiyan (PM-AASHA):

Description: PM-AASHA aims to ensure remunerative prices to farmers for their produce by providing procurement support and establishing market intelligence systems.
Website: Not available (Please refer to the Ministry of Agriculture and Farmers Welfare for more details)
