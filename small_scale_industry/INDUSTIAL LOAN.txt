Agriculture Infrastructure Fund (AIF):

Description: AIF provides medium to long-term debt financing for establishing and strengthening agricultural infrastructure, including post-harvest management, processing, and marketing.
Website: nabard.org/agri-infrastructure-fund.aspx

Pradhan Mantri Mudra Yojana (PMMY):

Description: PMMY provides loans for micro and small business enterprises, including those in the agricultural sector, facilitating their access to credit for establishing or expanding their businesses.
Website: mudra.org.in

National Bank for Agriculture and Rural Development (NABARD) Rural Entrepreneurship Development Program:

Description: The program aims to promote rural entrepreneurship by providing loans, skill development, and capacity-building support to rural individuals and groups.
Website: nabard.org

Stand-Up India Scheme:

Description: The scheme promotes entrepreneurship among women, Scheduled Castes (SCs), and Scheduled Tribes (STs) by providing loans for setting up greenfield enterprises, including those in the agricultural sector.
Website: standupmitra.in

National Small Industries Corporation (NSIC) Single Point Registration Scheme:

Description: The scheme facilitates credit support to small-scale industries, including those in the agricultural sector, by providing them with a Single Point Registration Certificate, enabling them to participate in government tenders.
Website: nsic.co.in
