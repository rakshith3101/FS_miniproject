Pradhan Mantri Kisan Sampada Yojana (PMKSY):

Description: PMKSY focuses on the development of modern infrastructure facilities for food processing, including the establishment of food parks, mega food parks, and integrated cold chains.
Website: pmkisan.gov.in/PmKisan/PMKSYHomePage.aspx

National Horticulture Mission (NHM):

Description: NHM aims to enhance horticultural productivity and promote holistic growth in the horticulture sector by providing support for the establishment of orchards, nurseries, and post-harvest infrastructure.
Website: nhm.nic.in

Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana (RKVY):

Description: RKVY focuses on promoting agricultural development through financial assistance for various activities, including the establishment of agribusiness centers, farm mechanization, and infrastructure development.
Website: rkvy.nic.in

Pradhan Mantri Formalization of Micro Food Processing Enterprises (PM-FME) Scheme:

Description: PM-FME aims to promote the formalization and capacity building of micro food processing enterprises by providing credit, technical support, and market access to enhance their competitiveness.
Website: mofpi.nic.in

Agri Export Policy:

Description: The Agri Export Policy aims to double agricultural exports from India by providing support for establishing agri-export zones, improving infrastructure, and enhancing market access for agricultural products.
Website: apeda.gov.in/apedawebsite/Agri_Export_Policy.htm
