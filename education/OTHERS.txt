Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY):

Description: A skill development program that aims to provide training and certification to enhance employability and entrepreneurship among youth across various industry sectors.
Website: pmkvyofficial.org

Beti Bachao Beti Padhao (BBBP):

Description: A campaign promoting the welfare and education of the girl child, focusing on addressing gender imbalance, improving the status of girls, and preventing gender-based discrimination.
Website: betibachaobetipadhao.co.in

National Literacy Mission:

Description: A program committed to achieving 100% literacy in India, providing opportunities for functional literacy and basic education to non-literate and neo-literate individuals.
Website: literacyind
