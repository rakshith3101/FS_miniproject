National Scholarship Portal:

Description: A portal that hosts various scholarships offered by the Indian government, providing a centralized platform for students to apply, track, and manage their scholarship applications.
Website: scholarships.gov.in

Maulana Azad National Scholarship:

Description: A scholarship scheme for minority students pursuing professional courses at the undergraduate and postgraduate levels, offering financial assistance to support their education.
Website: maef.nic.in

Post Matric Scholarship for OBC Students:

Description: A scholarship program aimed at providing financial support to students from Other Backward Classes (OBC) pursuing post-matriculation studies, helping them continue their education.
Website: scholarships.gov.in

Central Sector Scheme of Scholarship for College and University Students:

Description: A scholarship scheme for meritorious students from economically weaker sections, providing financial assistance to pursue higher education in recognized institutions.
Website: scholarships.gov.in

Prime Minister's Scholarship Scheme for Central Armed Police Forces and Assam Rifles:

Description: A scholarship scheme for the wards and widows of Central Armed Police Forces (CAPFs) and Assam Rifles personnel, providing financial assistance for their higher education.
Website: desw.gov.in
