Rashtriya Uchchatar Shiksha Abhiyan (RUSA):

Description: A scheme focusing on the development of higher education institutions, providing financial support to improve infrastructure, enhance quality, and increase access to higher education.
Website: rusa.gov.in

Samagra Shiksha Abhiyan:

Description: An integrated scheme for school education aiming to provide quality education and support holistic development of students, emphasizing inclusive education and infrastructure development.
Website: samagra.mhrd.gov.in

Pradhan Mantri Swasthya Suraksha Yojana (PMSSY):

Description: A program focused on the development of health infrastructure, including setting up AIIMS-like institutions, upgrading medical colleges, and improving healthcare facilities in underserved areas.
Website: pmssy-mohfw.nic.in

Atal Tinkering Labs:

Description: A scheme under the Atal Innovation Mission that promotes innovation and entrepreneurship among students by establishing tinkering labs in schools, providing hands-on learning opportunities.
Website: atalinnovationmission.com/atal-tinkering-labs

Digital India:

Description: An initiative that aims to transform India into a digitally empowered society, focusing on improving digital infrastructure, digital literacy, and promoting digital governance and services.
Website: digitalindia.gov.in
