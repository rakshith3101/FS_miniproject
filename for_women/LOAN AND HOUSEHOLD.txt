Pradhan Mantri MUDRA Yojana (PMMY):

Description: PMMY provides loans to micro and small enterprises for their business needs, supporting income generation and employment opportunities.
Website: mudra.org.in

Stand-Up India Scheme:

Description: The scheme facilitates bank loans between ₹10 lakh and ₹1 crore to Scheduled Caste (SC), Scheduled Tribe (ST), and women entrepreneurs for starting greenfield enterprises.
Website: standupmitra.in

Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (PMAY) - Credit-Linked Subsidy Scheme (CLSS):

Description: PMAY-CLSS provides interest subsidies on home loans for the construction, purchase, or improvement of houses, enabling affordable housing for economically weaker sections.
Website: pmaymis.gov.in

Pradhan Mantri Vaya Vandana Yojana (PMVVY):

Description: PMVVY is a pension scheme for senior citizens, offering a guaranteed return on investment and regular pension income to support their financial well-being during old age.
Website: licindia.in/Products/Pension-Plans/pradhan-mantri-vaya-vandana-yojana

Pradhan Mantri Jan Dhan Yojana (PMJDY):

Description: PMJDY promotes financial inclusion by providing basic banking services, including savings accounts, insurance, and access to credit, to unbanked individuals.
Website: pmjdy.gov.in
