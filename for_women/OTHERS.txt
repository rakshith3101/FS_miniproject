Beti Bachao Beti Padhao (BBBP) Scheme:

Description: BBBP aims to address gender discrimination and promote the education and welfare of girl children by raising awareness, enhancing access to education, and ensuring the protection of their rights.
Website: betibachaobetipadhao.gov.in

Pradhan Mantri Mahila Shakti Kendra (PMMSK):

Description: PMMSK supports the empowerment of rural women by providing skill development, entrepreneurship training, and assistance in accessing government schemes and services.
Website: wcd.nic.in/schemes/pradhan-mantri-mahila-shakti-kendra-pmmsk

Mahila e-Haat:

Description: Mahila e-Haat is an online platform that facilitates women entrepreneurs to showcase and sell their products, promoting economic empowerment and access to markets.
Website: mahilaehaat-rmk.gov.in

Ujjwala Scheme (Pradhan Mantri Ujjwala Yojana - PMUY):

Description: PMUY provides free LPG connections to women from below poverty line households, promoting clean cooking fuel, improving health, and empowering women.
Website: pmujjwalayojana.com

Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana Yojana (PMMVY):

Description: PMMVY provides financial assistance to pregnant and lactating women for their nutrition and health needs, promoting the well-being of mothers and children.
Website: pmmvy-cas.nic.in
